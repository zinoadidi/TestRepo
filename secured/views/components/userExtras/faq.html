FAQ's

What is PayDay Investor?

Payday Investor is the savings and investment app you need to help you meet your investment/savings goals. It is a channel that enables easy, targeted and personalised investment into the ARM Money Market Fund.

It not only helps you put money aside consistently, it invests the money on your behalf so that you are able to achieve your goal faster.

How does PayDay Investor work?

PayDay Investor allows you to select a savings goal and specify the time you would like to achieve that goal. It is designed to automatically connect to your Nigerian debit card accounts, thus making it easy for you to periodically set funds aside towards the goal.

PayDay Investor invests your funds in the ARM Money Market Fund, a mutual fund which guarantees quarterly returns than your traditional savings account. Leaving you more money to achieve your goal in good time.

All funds paid to your PayDay Investor Account are managed by ARM Investment Managers Limited, an asset management firm with over 23years of investment management experience and regulated by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), you are therefore guaranteed safety of your investments.

How do I access the PayDay Investor Channel?

You can download the PayDay Investor mobile app for free from your Android or iOS store. PayDay Investor is also accessible Online via www.paydayinvestor.ng.

To start using PayDay Investor, simply Register, Create a goal, Set up direct debits (optional) and Watch your investments grow.

Is there a limit to the amount I can invest?

No, there is no maximum limit to the money you can invest. Your savings depends on the size and number of goals you set. The ARM Money Market fund in which PayDay Investor invests allows you begin your investment with as little as N1,000 and make additional deposits of any amount you desire with a minimum limit of just N500.

How is PayDay Investor different from other Investing apps?

While other savings apps may help you save on the regular basis. PayDay Investor does much more. It not only compels you to consistently put money aside, invests your money on your behalf. Your money is managed by experienced investment managers such that you have more money than you put in.

What happens to the interest earned on my saving?

Interest returns are added to your PayDay Investor account every quarter. You can view - your accumulated earnings over the quarter on your PayDay Investor dashboard. At the end of the quarter, your account is credited with the interest. PayDay Investor allows you to watch your money grow daily. You can view interest earned.

Can I use PayDay Investor App on multiple devices?

Yes, you can. The app can be installed and managed on multiple devices. Your login details are confidential. Do NOT disclose your password to anyone.

What happens if my debit card account is not funded?

The scheduled debit from your account into your PayDay Investor account will not occur. We can only manage your money if your registered card account is funded. To ensure the automated debit feature works, your account must be funded on the scheduled day for payment. However, you can make unscheduled contribution payments to a PayDay Investor goal and the remaining payment period to achieve that goal will be automatically updated.

Can I make unscheduled contributions to my goal?

You can make additional unscheduled payments by selecting the goal and clicking the Top Up button.
